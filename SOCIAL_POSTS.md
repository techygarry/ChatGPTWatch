**I built ChatGPT for Apple Watch. And I'm open-sourcing it.**

After weeks of building, I'm releasing ChatGPT Watch — a full-featured ChatGPT client that runs natively on Apple Watch.

What it does:

- Chat with GPT-5.x models directly from your wrist
- Stream responses in real-time with typing indicators
- Listen to AI responses with voice output (OpenAI TTS)
- Run Codex coding tasks on your Mac remotely from the watch
- Voice input for hands-free conversations

Built with:

- SwiftUI + watchOS 26
- Apple's new Liquid Glass design
- OpenAI streaming API
- SwiftData for offline persistence
- Cloudflare Tunnels for the Codex relay

This isn't a wrapper or a notification mirror. It's a standalone watch app with full streaming chat, conversation history, model selection (GPT-5.4 through GPT-4o), and a Codex integration that lets you dispatch coding tasks to your Mac from your wrist.

The entire project — watch app, iPhone companion, widgets, relay server — is open source under MIT.

GitHub: https://github.com/techygarry/ChatGPTWatch

If you're into watchOS development, SwiftUI, or just want to talk to ChatGPT without pulling out your phone — give it a star and try it out.
